ABOUT ME

I am committed to providing the highest standard of medical care that you and your family deserve.

Dr. Satav holds an M.D. from the prestigious Harvard Medical School and has over 15 years of experience in internal medicine. He is a board-certified physician known for his patient-centric approach and dedication to clinical excellence.

He continuously engages in medical research and has published several papers on cardiovascular health. His commitment is to provide not just treatment, but also education and preventive care to his patients.
